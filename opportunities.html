Opportunities in the Corn Lab

Join the lab!

I am always interested in recruiting students and postdocs to my lab group! I encourage you to contact me if you are interested in the research that we are doing. I have positions open in my lab for undergraduates, graduate students pursuing a Masters' of Science or a PhD, as well as for postdoctoral research.

WSU in Pullman, Washington

The Corn Lab will be at Washington State University (WSU) in Pullman, Washington. Pullman is a college town in the Palouse in eastern Washington, a bit over 4 hrs' drive from Seattle, with excellent restaurants and easy access to beautiful outdoor recreation. We are very lucky to be part of the vibrant Palouse ecology and evolution community.

Washington state is an excellent place to live, with strong legal protections for women, people of color, and the LGBTQIA2S+ community.

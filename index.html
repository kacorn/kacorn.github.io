Welcome to the website for the Corn Lab at Washington State University. I am an evolutionary biologist, biomechanist, and fish enthusiast. My lab group studies how the different ways that animals interact with their physical environment can result in different evolutionary patterns over multi-million-year scales. We often use the evolution of prey capture, often in reef fishes, as a study system.
